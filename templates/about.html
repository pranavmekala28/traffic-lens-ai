What this is

About Traffic Lens

A local-first tool that turns traffic footage into two kinds of answers: how many vehicles, and where the crashes are.

Detection engine

YOLOv8 + OpenCV

Counts cars, trucks, buses, motorcycles, and bicycles frame by frame, and writes an annotated video with boxes drawn on every vehicle.

Collision engine

Jockey

Reads the footage semantically, finds the moments vehicles impact each other, and returns each one with a start/end timestamp and a plain-language description.

How a video moves through it
  1. Upload — drop a road or dashcam clip into the browser.
  2. Detect — YOLOv8 boxes and counts every vehicle, and saves an annotated copy.
  3. Analyze — Jockey indexes the clip and scans it for collisions.
  4. Read — results land in the dashboard, the collision timeline, and a report.
